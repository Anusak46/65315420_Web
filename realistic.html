Hand Poses Right

HAELE 3D - Hand Poser is a 3D anatomy reference app that comes with a variety of presets but, most importantly, allows you to adjust hands to achieve the pose you need.

You can change skin tones, backgrounds, lighting, camera settings, and materials to get the perfect hands for your next project, be it a game, a 2D drawing, a 3D model, an animation, or anything else. Make the hands small, big, young, old, wet, or even made of gold.

Impressive Pose Detection Setup Powered by Houdini & AI

Powered by a combination of SideFX's flagship procedural 3D software, Houdini, and AI, the framework can analyze video footage to detect the number of fingers being held up and display the result using particles in Houdini.
